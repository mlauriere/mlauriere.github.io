Mathieu Laurière

Visiting Faculty Researcher

Google Brain, Brain Team



I am currently a Visiting Faculty Researcher at Google Brain, in the Brain Team (Paris). I obtained my MS from University Paris 6 and ENS Cachan, and my PhD from University Paris 7. Prior to joining Google Brain, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the NYU-ECNU Institute of Mathematical Sciences at NYU Shanghai and a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Princeton University, in the Operations Research and Financial Engineering (ORFE) department.

My research interests include mean field control and mean field games, numerical methods, partial differential equations, stochastic analysis, machine learning, complexity theory and quantum computing.
